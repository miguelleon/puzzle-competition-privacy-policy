1. Introduction

LeonAI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Puzzle Competition mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Data: If you choose to synchronize your progress with Google, we may collect your Google User ID, email address, and profile name to manage your account and save your progress.
  • Anonymous identifier: For users not signed in, we assign a randomly generated anonymous ID to track game scores and leaderboards.
  • Game data: Scores, game history, and progress are stored and associated with your account or anonymous ID.
  • Advertising data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate leaderboards and track competitive scores.
  • To provide account synchronization across devices.
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To improve the App's functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services / Firebase — authentication and cloud data storage. Privacy Policy

5. Data Deletion and Retention

How to delete your account and data:

We provide two ways to exercise your right to delete your data:

  • In-App: You can find a "Delete Account" button within the App settings menu to instantly remove your data.
  • Via Web/Email: If you have uninstalled the App and wish to delete your data, please contact us at puzzlecompetitionapp@gmail.com.

Upon request, we will permanently delete your user profile, scores, and all synchronization data from our databases within 7 business days.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at puzzlecompetitionapp@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
